Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and learning platform.

What is the duration of each course?

Most of our courses are designed to be completed in 8 weeks with a commitment of 10-15 hours per week. However, you can learn at your own pace as all course materials remain accessible even after completion.

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

Yes, upon successful completion of any course, you'll receive a verifiable digital certificate that you can share on LinkedIn and other professional networks. Some courses also offer additional industry-recognized certifications.

What kind of support can I expect during the course?

You'll have access to multiple support channels including 1:1 mentorship sessions, community forums, live Q&A sessions, and email support. Our instructors and mentors are committed to helping you succeed.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all our courses. If you're not satisfied with your learning experience within the first two weeks, you can request a full refund with no questions asked.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive career support including resume reviews, interview preparation, and job placement assistance. We also have partnerships with several tech companies that hire directly from our talent pool.
